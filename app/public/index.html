About Loo Locator

Whether you have Crohn's, Colitis, IBS or a you just want to find a nice clean public restroom near you or wherever you’re headed, Loo-Locator is here to help! All bathrooms are crowdsourced entries and ratings are based on the submitter’s personal opinion. Loo-Locator | Bean Redheads assumes no legal liability for content and makes assertions to the accuracy of the information provided by our users.
